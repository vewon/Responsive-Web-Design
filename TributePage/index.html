V in the Afterlife

Reliving the Legend's Story

V, alongside her partner Jackie Welles, embarked on their first major assignment, a job from a fixer, Dexter DeShawn.

Their mission led them to infiltrate Konpeki Plaza, where they successfully stole a Relic from the powerful Arasaka corporation.

To prevent further damage to the stolen Relic, V inserted the shard into her neural interface.

In a shocking turn of events, Dexter DeShawn shot V. However, the Relic brought her back to life.

This event led to V's fateful encounter with Johnny Silverhand.

V continued her journey as a mercenary, eventually earning the reputation as the best merc in the Afterlife.

She eventually left Night City alongside Judy Alvarez.
